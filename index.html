Why compromise between artistry and technology?

Corpus Callosum is a student organization that encourages creative engineering.

We're one of Viterbi's most diverse student orgs.

Over half of our 70 members are women.

Our students range from freshman to graduate,

coming from every USC school.

This year, we ambitiously pushed our students to use cutting edge technology.

Virtual reality + Poetry

Computer Vision + Dance

IoT + Skateboarding

Chemical Engineering + Painting

Electrical Engineering + Sculpture

Robotics + Sociology

All of our projects are brainstormed, drafted, and prototyped over semester-long project cycles.

The final showcase is the culmination of a semester of learning and hard work.

We invite tech professionals, art studios, academic staff, and our peers to celebrate art, technology, and creativity with us.
