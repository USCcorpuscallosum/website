what we do

Corpus Callosum is named after the physiological bridge in the brain - the piece that combines logic with art. Each semester students from every USC school break into groups to create unique projects that combine art and technology which are displayed at our final showcase. We focus on learning and creative exploration using cutting-edge technologies.
